G1 — THE NORTH STAR RULE
RULE 1 — CHECK DXY FIRST, ALWAYS
Before any trade: check DXY direction in MACRO tab.
DXY UP = only look for USD strength plays (SELL EUR/USD, SELL XAU/USD, BUY USD/JPY)
DXY DOWN = only look for USD weakness plays (BUY EUR/USD, BUY XAU/USD, SELL USD/JPY)
THEN: Cross-reference with S&P risk gauge for full picture
G2 — INSTITUTIONAL FLOW (COT)
RULE 2 — FOLLOW INSTITUTIONAL MONEY
COT NET LONG = institutions positioned bullish (buy bias this week)
COT NET SHORT = institutions positioned bearish (sell bias this week)
COT lag is 3-8 days. Use as weekly directional bias, not daily signal.
RULE 3 — COT vs PRICE DIVERGENCE
If COT says NET LONG but price is falling — institutions are accumulating.
THEN: Wait for price to find support, then look for buys
G3 — RISK SENTIMENT (S&P 500)
RULE 4 — S&P CONFIRMS DIRECTION
S&P RISING + DXY FALLING = Maximum risk-on. AUD/USD, NZD/USD, Gold all bullish.
S&P FALLING + DXY RISING = Maximum risk-off. USD, JPY, CHF safe havens.
THEN: Full position size when both DXY and S&P agree
RULE 5 — CONFLICT = REDUCE SIZE
S&P rising BUT DXY also rising = conflicting signals, unusual environment.
THEN: Half size only. Something unusual is driving one of them.
G4 — NEWS EVENTS
RULE 6 — NEWS LOCKOUT
HIGH IMPACT event within 15 minutes = NO NEW TRADES.
Wait for the candle after the news to close before assessing direction.
THEN: Let the market absorb the news first.
RULE 7 — BEAT vs MISS
Data BEATS forecast = strengthens that currency (USD data beats = USD up)
Data MISSES forecast = weakens that currency
THEN: Check NEWS tab — actual vs forecast tells you direction post-event
